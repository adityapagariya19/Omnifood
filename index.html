How it works

Your daily dose of health, in three simple steps

01

Tell us what you like — and what you don't

Never waste time deciding what to eat again. Omnifood AI builds a 100% personalized weekly plan for you, balancing every nutrient and vitamin you need, whatever diet you follow.

02

Approve your weekly meal plan

Once a week, review the plan Omnifood AI put together for you. Swap ingredients, replace whole meals, or drop in your own recipes — the plan adapts instantly.

03

Receive your meals, right on time

Local chefs cook your selected meals fresh, every day, and we deliver them whenever suits you best. Change your delivery window or address any time.
